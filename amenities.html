Amenities

TENNIS COURTS

DecoTurf Indoor Tennis Courts

You will feel like tennis royalty when you step out onto any one of our four DecoTurf indoor courts. Surrounded by glass, views of Lake Ontario and downtown Toronto and 50-foot ceilings, these indoor courts were designed to make you feel like you’re playing outdoors with the benefits of indoor comfort. Our courts are surfaced by Tennex - the same team responsible for surfacing the courts at the Roger’s Cup every year.

Opportunities for Hotel Guests:

  • Tennis Lessons
  • Tennis Clinics
    • Instructional programs for kids and adults to learn the game of tennis
  • Tennis Events

SQUASH COURTS

9 Glass Back Squash Courts (7 singles & 2 doubles)

Our facility boasts a total of 9 squash courts – 7 International singles courts and 2 North American doubles courts. Each court is constructed by industry leaders McWIL Squash Inc and features composite panel front walls, glass backed walls, as well as hardwood floors with shock-absorbing subfloors.

Opportunities for Hotel Guests:

  • Squash Lessons
  • Squash Clinics

HD GOLF SIMULATORS

2 HD golf simulators

We have two golf simulators offered from High Definition Golf – the top choice for golf instructors and top golfers around the world. Special image processing software integrates high-resolution digital images, satellite data and geophysical data into three-dimensional models of golf courses that play just like the real course. We offer both instructional and play opportunities to meet all needs. For more information,visit their website - http://hdgolf.com

FITNESS CENTRE

Equipped by Technogym’s Artis collection

Our fitness centre is lined with top-of-the-line fitness equipment from Technogym’s Artis collection. We offer over 50 machines ranging from cardio, strength and functionality equipment. Each machine is methodically placed to ensure a flawless workout routine. Can’t wait to see these pieces? Get a sneak peak here - http://www.technogym.com/artis/en

STUDIOS

4 Fitness Studios

At TEN X TORONTO, we have four fitness studios including spinning, hot yoga, pilates and group fitness. Our group fitness classes will include the common fan-favourites such as group power, cardio blast, circuit training and more, as well as a number of proprietary classes. With a large group fitness space, we are flexible to class structures and offerings. It is our promise to be continuously evolving our class offerings to stay up-to-date with the latest trends and to ensure all fitness needs are met. In addition to our hot yoga studio, we offer morning and evening yoga session during the spring and summer months out on our beautiful East Terrace overlooking the downtown core (weather permitting).
